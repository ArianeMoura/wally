# Design System

Wally's visual identity conveys practicality, modernity and trust. This document
consolidates the fundamentals — logo, colours, typography and components — that keep
the product consistent.

## Logo

<div align="center"><img width="300" alt="Wally logo" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b771c1a8-d771-4380-834a-ad678e378283" /></div>

- **Concept:** the name echoes _wallet_. The logo is a stylised wallet, reinforcing
  control and financial organisation.
- **Shape:** soft, fluid lines suggesting agility and ease of use.
- **Dollar-sign icon:** underlines the product's financial purpose.

## Colours

<div align="center"><img width="560" alt="Colour palette" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/38a875f7-4eed-45a7-bae0-decb9789675e" /></div>

The palette is built on **teal and turquoise** variations:

- **Green** — associated with money, prosperity and growth; conveys balance and
  stability.
- **Teal / turquoise** — a sense of modernity and innovation, fresh and approachable.
- Contrast between shades establishes the visual hierarchy, surfacing the most
  important information first.

## Typography

| Use                   | Typeface            | Application                                        |
| --------------------- | ------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Logo                  | **Julius Sans One** | Brand signature                                    |
| Headings and emphasis | **Poppins**         | Titles, subtitles and highlighted elements         |
| Body copy             | **Inter**           | Descriptions and long-form content, highly legible |

## Illustration

Minimalist icons and illustrations with simple strokes and rounded, organic shapes,
following the primary palette to keep every touchpoint visually coherent.

<div align="center"><img width="260" alt="Illustration" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/707926bb-6313-46b3-9708-e616ca2edbae" /></div>

## Layout guidelines

- **Minimalist layout** — a clean design that uses whitespace deliberately to let charts
  and financial figures stand out.
- **Visual hierarchy** — Poppins for headings, Inter for body copy, Julius Sans One for
  the logo.
- **Buttons and actions** — follow the green palette, with primary actions emphasised.
